Industrial Visit to Rajasthan was conducted for Third Year Civil Students from 11/12/2019 to 19/12/2019 to study architect design of historic monuments, site visit at luxurious bungalow at Jaipur to study architectural planning and design, site visit at WTP at Jodhpur to study various operational units and its functions and importance of water conservation.

Industrial Visits 19 20 28 25
  • Site visits of SE Civil students to construction site of Proposed Talera Hospital Building Chinchwad Pune under subject APDB on 01 st April 2019 to study public building (hospital) functional requirement and building design consideration.
  • Site visits of SE Civil students to M/s Kalokhe RMC, Talegaon near village Ambi under subject CT on 16 th March 2019 to study process of fully automated RMC Plant.
  • Site visits of SE Civil students to Talegaon, near village Ambi under subject EG on 16 th March 2019 to study different Basalts in field, joints, and fractures in Basalts, Grouting and Bolting.
  • Site visits of SE Civil students in batches (S1, S2, S3 and S4) to construction site of Polite Precisa (G+12) residential project under subject APDB from 14 th to 22 nd February 2019 to study GHIRA Compliant homes for smart energy saving.
  • Site visits of TE Civil students to Casa Grande by Dolphin Group of Construction Ravet Pune under subject SD-II on 26th March 2019 to study reinforcement details of structural members.
  • Site visits of TE Civil students to Parvatara by Saakshi Constructions, Ravet, Pune under subject SD-II on 1 st February 2019 to study reinforcement details of structural members.
  • Site visits of TE Civil students to Water Treatment Plant, Nigdi, Pune under subject EE-I on 18 th and 19 th January 2019 to study unit operation and process of water treatment plant.
  • Site visits of BE Civil students to “Sant Tukaram Sahakari Sahkari Sakhar Karkhana Ltd., Mulshi” under subject APC on 23 rd March 2019 to study the process of electricity generation with baggase as a fuel.
  • Site visits of BE students to “Urmodi Dam and Tarali Dam, Power House, Satara Irrigation Project Circle, Dist Satara. on 22 nd February 2019 under subject DHS to study Earthen and gravity dam constructional details, various components, Power house , canal structures, Energy Dissipation Device.
  • Site visits of BE students to “PCCoE, Nigdi campus new construction site” on 27 th March 2019 under subject CM to study Project cash flow analysis, Project balance sheet work break down structure, materials flow system in the project.
