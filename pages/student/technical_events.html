Student Development And Welfare

We at Civil Engineering Department of PCCoE ensure the overall development of students by conducting various social and professional activities which strengthen and enrich the community within the department. The strong team of faculties and students work together to enhance the knowledge and awareness in the field of Civil Engineering through guest lectures, workshops, co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

Technical Events


Launch of YouTube Channel

We happy to announce that we recently started our own YouTube channel on 12th March 2021. The reason behind this decision is that we want to share our views, insights, and knowledge not only on this blog but also in video format.

Ankahi Batten

“Ankahi Batten” – 14th March 2021 Open Mic session open to all students of PCCOE. Students performed shayari, poem, song, stand up comedy, story telling, experience sharing. No language barrier.

Faculty Coordinator at department - Mr. S. P. Gaikwad


Launch of Telegram Channel

We are glad to announce that we have created our telegram channel on 15th March 2021. A Telegram channel is a power-charged version of the WhatsApp Broadcast list and it is a medium for one-way broadcast messages. A Telegram group is similar to any other chat group where people can interact by sending messages.

Faculty Coordinator at department - Mr. S. P. Gaikwad
